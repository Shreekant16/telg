Blood Test Guide

This guide helps you get a blood test at a nearby pathology centre to check hormone levels and other health markers. The blood test should be testing the following parameters:

  • Hormones (FSH, LH, Prolactin, Testosterone)
  • Thyroid Function (T3, T4, TSH)
  • Blood Sugar (Fasting & After Eating)
  • Insulin (Fasting & After Eating)

Before You Go:

[IMPORTANT NOTE: These tests should be done during the follicular phase and in non-pregnant condition] To check whether you are in Follicular phase Refer the menstrual tracking feature in our application.

  • Fasting: Avoid food or sugary drinks for at least 8 hours before the test (water is okay).
  • Medications: Let the lab know if you take any medications.
